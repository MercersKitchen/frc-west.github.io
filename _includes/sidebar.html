FRC West Curriculum

Courses designed for FRC team members by FRC members to educate students about the concepts used in engineering, design, programming, fabrication and more.

FRC West is proud to make our courses publicly available for use by anyone. Feel free to use, distribute and teach our materials. They are licensed under the GPLv3.

We would appreciate if you used these courses under ADLC, as it helps fund our program. You are under no obligation to, however.
